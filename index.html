About Our School

PM Shri Scheme Vision

PM Shri Primary School Tigri is part of the prestigious PM SHRI (Pradhan Mantri Schools for Rising India) scheme. We aim to be an exemplary school that offers leadership to other schools in the neighborhood.

We provide a safe, stimulating, and high-quality learning environment. Our focus is not just on textbooks, but on creating well-rounded individuals who are confident, creative, and compassionate.

  • Modern Smart Classrooms
  • Focus on NEP 2020
  • Experiential Learning Pedagogy
